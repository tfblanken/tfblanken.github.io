Our paper <a href="https://pure.knaw.nl/portal/files/9406926/Blanken2019Repository_Insomnia_Subtypes_Manuscript.pdf5" target="blank">on insomnia subtypes</a> got published online January 8th 2019 in *The Lancet Psychiatry*.

After the paper got published online, newspapers, online magazines and platforms from countries around the world discussed our paper: Argentina, Australia, Belgium, Cuba, France, Germany, Hungary, Indonesia, Israel, Italy, Mexico, Netherlands, Poland, Portugal, Spain, Ukrain, UK, USA.


All 75 media items on the paper can be found <a href="https://pure.knaw.nl/portal/en/clippings/meer-helderheid-in-slapeloosheid(144caf18-50c7-48b1-a7a5-0ba90281e831).html">here</a>.

some highlights:

Nemo Kennislink interviewed me on the paper and wrote a nice <a href="https://www.nemokennislink.nl/publicaties/vijf-typen-slapeloosheid-verborgen-in-karaktereigenschappen/">article</a> about it (in Dutch). 


It reached a large audience through <a href="https://www.reddit.com/r/science/comments/ahruy8/there_are_five_types_of_insomnia_each_with_its/">r/science</a> where it got more than 29000 upvotes.
