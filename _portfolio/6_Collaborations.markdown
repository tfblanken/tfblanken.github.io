During my PhD I had the privilege to collaborate with many different researchers from different research labs and fields. 

insomnia and depression


- To identify data-driven subtypes of insomnia disorder we used latent class analysis, for which we collaborated with <a href="https://jeroenvermunt.nl/" target="blank">Jeroen Vermunt</a> (Tilburg University).

- To identify sequential and symptom-specific treatment effects we worked together with <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/tanja-van-der-zweerde-4b1b4a40/?originalSubdomain=nl" target="blank">Tanja van der Zweerde</a> (VU University), <a href="https://research.vu.nl/en/persons/annemieke-van-straten" target="blank">Annemieke van Straten</a> (VU University) and <a href="https://www.uva.nl/profiel/l/a/j.lancee/j.lancee.html" target="blank">Jaap Lancee</a> (University of Amsterdam).

<br/>

community detection and graph theory

- I have worked closely together with Marie Deserno to explore community detection and graph theory. In our first project we collaborated closely together with  <a href="https://jdalege.com/" target="blank">Jonas Dalege</a> (University of Amsterdam) and <a href="https://www.aojcramer.com/" target="blank">Angélique Cramer</a> (Tilburg University) to explore the community structure of a large psychopathology network. 

- Afterwards we joined a project led by <a href="https://srebrenkaletina.com/" target="blank">Srebrenka Letina</a> (Central European University, Budapest, Hungary), in which we introduced the applicability of graph theory metrics in psychological networks. 

- In a project together with <a href="https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Thomas_Villemonteix" target="blank">Thomas Villemonteix</a> (University 8, Paris, France), we applied community detection to investigate subtypes of ADHD based on their personality profiles

<br/>


sleep as dynamical system

- Together with <a href="https://www.uva.nl/profiel/m/o/s.m.m.demooij/s.m.m.de-mooij.html" target="blank">Susanne de Mooij</a> and <a href="http://hvandermaas.socsci.uva.nl/Homepage_Han_van_der_Maas/Home.html" target="blank">Han van der Maas</a> we explored the possibility to approach sleep (stages) as a dynamical system.
